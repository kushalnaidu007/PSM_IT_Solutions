Warranty Claims
To submit a warranty claim, you must provide valid proof of repair, such as the original receipt. Spondon Tech Solutions reserves the right to inspect the device to determine whether the warranty applies.

Covered Under Warranty
- Manufacturer defects (electrical or mechanical faults)
- Faults in new parts within the stated warranty period

Not Covered Under Warranty
- Any damage occurring after the repair
- Damage caused by misuse
- Any form of liquid damage
- Software-related issues
- Faults unrelated to the original repair
- Loss of data (please back up your device before repair)
- Devices opened or repaired by another company after our repair
- Screens replaced by us that suffer internal pressure damage without visible cracks or physical damage are not covered under warranty

Refunds / Non-Refundable Repairs
Some repairs are non-refundable, including liquid-damage repairs, liquid-damage-related repairs, and diagnostic fees if you choose not to proceed with the repair.
Refunds are only issued if we are unable to fix your device. We provide up to three warranty repair attempts. If the issue remains after the third attempt, we will remove our parts and issue a full refund (a minimum fee and any call-out or postage charges will still apply).

The 1-Year Warranty does not apply if the device has suffered accidental damage. Accidental damage includes, but is not limited to:

- Mishandling that causes further damage

- Software issues unrelated to the repair, including viruses or malicious software
- Jailbroken or “rooted” devices
- Waterproof devices, as we cannot guarantee waterproofing after repair
